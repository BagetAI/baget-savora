Zero Plastic Footprint

High barrier protection. Zero lasting waste.

Ground spices are vulnerable to moisture, oxygen, and UV light, which dissipate essential flavors. Standard bio-plastics let oils pass through.

Savora uses a proprietary certified compostable three-layer cellulose laminate (NatureFlex NK). It matches the oxygen barrier of aluminum foil but decomposes naturally in your backyard compost heap in 26 weeks.
