MAPO TOFU - FRONT
TIME: 20 MINS SERVES: 4 LEVEL: EASY

Sichuan Mapo Tofu

The classic numbing and spicy comfort bowl.

In Your Savora Kit

  • Erjingtiao Chili Flakes
  • Red Sichuan Peppercorns
  • Douchi (Fermented Beans)
  • Ginger & Garlic Blend

From Your Kitchen

  • 1 lb Ground Pork or Mushrooms
  • 1 block Silken Tofu
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
SAVORA // GLOBAL FLAVOR, EXACT PORTIONS
MAPO TOFU - BACK

Instructions

01
PREP THE HEAT: Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan. Toast the whole Savora Peppercorns for 30s until fragrant. Remove with a slotted spoon if you prefer a smoother texture, or leave them in for the authentic crunch.
02
BROWN & SEASON: Add the ground meat or mushrooms. Cook until browned. Stir in the remaining Savora Spice Blend and 1 tbsp soy sauce. Cook for 1 minute until the oil turns a vibrant red.
03
SIMMER: Gently slide in the cubed silken tofu and 1/2 cup water. Simmer for 5 mins, spooning the sauce over the tofu. Finish with a drizzle of sesame oil or scallions. Serve over white rice.
Chef's Tip: For extra silkiness, blanch your tofu cubes in lightly salted boiling water for 1 minute before adding them to the pan. This prevents them from breaking during the simmer.

JOLLOF RICE - FRONT
TIME: 35 MINS SERVES: 4 LEVEL: MEDIUM

West African Jollof

Party-style rice with deep smoke and umami.

In Your Savora Kit

  • Nigerian-style Curry Powder
  • High-Umami Bouillon
  • Dried Thyme & Bay Leaf
  • Smoked Paprika

From Your Kitchen

  • 2 cups Long-grain Rice
  • 1 large Red Onion (diced)
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 3 cups Water or Tomato Puree
SAVORA // GLOBAL FLAVOR, EXACT PORTIONS
JOLLOF RICE - BACK

Instructions

01
THE BASE: Sauté the diced onion in 3 tbsp oil until soft. Add the Savora Spice Blend and 1 tbsp tomato paste. Fry for 2-3 mins until the paste deepens in color and the spices are fragrant.
02
COAT THE GRAINS: Stir in 2 cups of rinsed long-grain rice. Mix well to ensure every grain is coated in the red oil. This "toasting" step ensures the rice doesn't get mushy.
03
THE STEAM: Add 3 cups of water (or tomato puree). Bring to a boil, then reduce to the lowest heat. Cover tightly with foil and a lid. Simmer for 25 mins. Turn off heat and let it steam for 10 mins. Do not stir!
Chef's Tip: The "bottom of the pot" (the burnt bits) is called "socarrat" in some cultures—in West Africa, it's the sign of a perfect Jollof. Don't be afraid of a little char at the base.

SUMAC FISH - FRONT
TIME: 15 MINS SERVES: 4 LEVEL: EASY

Levantine Sumac Fish

Bright, zesty fish with a nutty sesame crust.

In Your Savora Kit

  • Pure Ground Sumac
  • Toasted White Sesame
  • Wild Thyme & Za'atar Mix
  • Aleppo Pepper Flakes

From Your Kitchen

  • 4 White Fish Fillets (Cod/Bass)
  • 1 Lemon (Juice & Zest)
  • Olive Oil
  • Sea Salt
SAVORA // GLOBAL FLAVOR, EXACT PORTIONS
SUMAC FISH - BACK

Instructions

01
PREP: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat your fish fillets bone-dry with paper towels. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt.
02
CRUST: Press the Savora Za'atar & Sumac Blend generously onto the top surface of each fillet. You want a thick, purple-flecked crust that will toast in the oven.
03
ROAST: Bake for 12-15 mins depending on thickness. The fish should flake easily with a fork and the sesame seeds should be golden. Serve with a side of lemony couscous or a fresh salad.
Chef's Tip: Sumac loses its bright tartness if cooked too long. If you want an extra punch, sprinkle a tiny pinch of the raw sumac over the fish right before serving.

MASSAMAN - FRONT
TIME: 30 MINS SERVES: 4 LEVEL: MEDIUM

Thai Massaman Curry

Warm, aromatic spices in a rich coconut base.

In Your Savora Kit

  • Massaman Dry-Spice Base
  • Bird's Eye Chili Flakes
  • Whole Star Anise & Cardamom
  • Coconut Sugar Blend

From Your Kitchen

  • 1 lb Chicken or Beef
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 1 large Potato (cubed)
  • 1 Shallot (sliced)
SAVORA // GLOBAL FLAVOR, EXACT PORTIONS
MASSAMAN - BACK

Instructions

01
INFUSE: Sauté sliced shallot and meat in oil for 5 mins. Stir in the Savora Spice Blend. Cook for 2 mins, allowing the warm spices (cinnamon/star anise) to bloom in the oil.
02
SIMMER: Pour in the coconut milk and add the cubed potato. Bring to a gentle simmer. Cover and cook for 20 mins until the potatoes are fork-tender and the meat is cooked through.
03
BALANCE: Stir in the included coconut sugar to balance the heat. Taste and add a splash of fish sauce or salt if needed. Garnish with peanuts and serve with jasmine rice.
Chef's Tip: Massaman is a "Persian-influenced" Thai curry, which is why it uses warm spices like cinnamon. It's even better the next day after the spices have fully melded.

OAXACAN TACOS - FRONT
TIME: 15 MINS SERVES: 4 LEVEL: EASY

Oaxacan Taco Rub

Deep, smoky chile flavor with a hint of cacao.

In Your Savora Kit

  • Ancho & Guajillo Chile Rub
  • Mexican Oregano & Cumin
  • Pure Oaxacan Cacao Powder
  • Smoked Paprika

From Your Kitchen

  • 1 lb Ground Beef or Crumbles
  • Corn Tortillas
  • 1 Lime
  • Fresh Cilantro & Onion
SAVORA // GLOBAL FLAVOR, EXACT PORTIONS
OAXACAN TACOS - BACK

Instructions

01
BROWN: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the meat or plant-based crumbles until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat if necessary.
02
DEEPEN: Sprinkle the Savora Oaxacan Rub over the meat. Add 1/4 cup water and a squeeze of lime. Stir well and simmer for 3 mins until the sauce is glossy and the cacao has melted into the chiles.
03
ASSEMBLE: Serve in warm corn tortillas. Top with finely diced raw white onion, fresh cilantro, and an extra squeeze of lime. Simple, smoky, and authentic.
Chef's Tip: The tiny amount of cacao in this rub doesn't make it sweet—it adds the "Mole" complexity and a rich, dark color that defines Oaxacan cooking.
